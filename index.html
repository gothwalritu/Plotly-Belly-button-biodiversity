Overview of the project

Many bactreial spcies are still unknown to us. In the belly button biodiversity project, the diversity of bacterial species in the belly buttons of the humans has been studied and documented. The sample were collected from the volunteers. This webpage presents a dashboard that shows the demographic information for each volunteer and also shows the graphical presentation of the top ten bacterial species in their belly buttons.
